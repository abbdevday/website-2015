Conference Centre Faculty of Medicine JUMC

16 Sw. Lazarza Str., PL 31-530 Cracow

  • By car

    Cracow is well situated next to the A4 motorway that goes from West to East. Try to avoid rush hours as traffic can be troublesome. Parking places are fairly easy to find, even in the venue's neighbourhood.

  • By train

    There are very good connections from Wroclaw, Lodz and Warsaw. The venue is literally 15 minutes walk from Kraków Główny railway station.

  • By plane

    Cracow has the second biggest airport in Poland, hence it's very well connected with many big european ones. The easiest way to get to the city centre from the airport is by taxi (~90PLN) or by bus.

Be advised that workshops are going to be held in a different location than the conference itself. Check workshops for details.
